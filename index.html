About Me

I'm a Digital Product Leader with over 15 years of experience spanning gaming, fintech, travel tech, and digital commerce. My career has taken me from building Facebook games at Innogames, through pioneering mobile payments at Yapital (OTTO Group), to driving digital transformation at AIDA Cruises — where I discovered the power of NLP and AI for customer insights. Today, I lead digital product development at Gebr. Heinemann, steering everything from webshops and apps to CRM and personalization with Salesforce.

What drives me is the intersection of strategy and innovation. I love turning complex challenges into elegant digital solutions, building empowered teams, and staying ahead of what's next in tech. Currently, I'm deeply exploring AI Agents and Automation — because I believe the best products come from combining human creativity with AI capabilities.

When I'm not building products, you'll find me on the padel court, traveling, or diving into the latest AI developments.
